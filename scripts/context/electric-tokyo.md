# Electric Tokyo

Shibuya at night after rain. Neon reflected in wet asphalt — hot pink, electric blue, amber. Vending machines glowing on quiet side streets. The contrast between dense visual noise and moments of stillness.

Typography: condensed katakana on vertical signs, pixel grids, sharp geometry softened by atmospheric haze.

Palette: deep indigo night sky, neon magenta, warm amber glow, rain-slick charcoal.
