Nordstrom is known for exceptional customer service and curated selection. The challenge was translating that in-store experience to digital platforms while maintaining the brand's premium positioning.

## The Challenge

E-commerce design for a luxury retailer requires balancing conversion optimization with brand experience. Users needed to be able to shop efficiently, but the experience also needed to feel premium and aligned with Nordstrom's brand values.

## The Approach

The design work emphasized quality imagery, thoughtful curation, and seamless shopping experiences. The interface needed to feel sophisticated while remaining functional and accessible across devices.

## Impact

Working with Nordstrom provided insights into how established retail brands approach digital transformation. The work reinforced the importance of maintaining brand consistency across channels while optimizing for digital behaviors.
