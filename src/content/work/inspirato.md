Inspirato was a luxury travel platform that reimagined how people discover and book premium vacation experiences. The work involved designing interfaces that matched the quality and sophistication of the travel experiences being offered.

![Inspirato Platform Overview](/images/work/inspirato/img_portfolio_inspirato_01.jpg)

## The Challenge

Designing for luxury travel requires balancing elegance with functionality. Users needed to be able to browse and book seamlessly, but the experience also needed to feel premium and curated—reflecting the high-end nature of the properties and experiences.

![Inspirato Property Discovery](/images/work/inspirato/img_portfolio_inspirato_02.jpg)

## The Approach

The design focused on creating a sense of discovery and aspiration. High-quality imagery was central to the experience, allowing users to visualize themselves in these luxury spaces. The interface needed to feel refined without being intimidating.

![Inspirato Booking Experience](/images/work/inspirato/img_portfolio_inspirato_03.jpg)

![Inspirato Interface Details](/images/work/inspirato/img_portfolio_inspirato_04.jpg)

The visual design emphasized spacious layouts, premium typography, and carefully curated photography that showcased the luxury properties. Every interaction was designed to feel effortless and refined, matching the expectations of a high-end travel experience.

![Inspirato Design System](/images/work/inspirato/img_portfolio_inspirato_05.jpg)

## Impact

The work at Inspirato reinforced the importance of understanding your audience and designing experiences that match their expectations. For luxury products, every detail matters—from typography choices to interaction patterns.
